# Contributing

Contributions are welcome through issues and pull requests.

## Before proposing a change

- Open an issue for major changes to the lecture's scope or structure.
- Cite a primary or authoritative source for factual or data changes.
- Keep the deck in English and accessible to international students who may not know Brazilian institutions.
- Distinguish organized crime, lethal violence, and territorial control rather than treating them as interchangeable measures.

## Pull requests

1. Fork the repository and create a focused branch.
2. Make the smallest change that addresses the issue.
3. Rebuild any affected figures and update `SOURCES.md` when the underlying data change.
4. Compile the deck twice and inspect the affected slides for clipping or scaling problems.
5. Describe the change and its source in the pull request.

Do not commit LaTeX auxiliary files, local environments, credentials, or proprietary data.
